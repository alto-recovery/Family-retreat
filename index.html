Course Instructor

Anastasia
Kalashnikova

Massage therapist and wellness specialist. Certified through the School of Massage and Cosmetology (classical and anti-cellulite massage) and Andrey Brodsky's courses (relaxation and sports massage). With extensive hands-on experience, she specialises in relaxation, recovery, and body awareness work.

Alto Academy is Anastasia's original course series — built on her belief that great technique starts with understanding, not just repetition. Before massage, she spent five years teaching English and worked at Art. Lebedev Studio. She knows how to break down complex things simply and create a genuinely supportive learning environment.
